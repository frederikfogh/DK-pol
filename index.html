This website lets you to compare statistics about Facebook advertisements by Dutch political parties. It is based on data from the Facebook Ad Library. For more information visit the about page.

Ads

All parties combined ran 34283 ads.

Spending

In total an between €2318600 - €5972017 has been spent.
The most expensive ad cost an estimated €4166.5 per day, ran for 3 day(s) and is from D66.

Impressions

These graphs show data about the impressions (i.e. how many times an ad was show).

Potential reach

This graph shows the potential reach (i.e. the maximum amount of people that could have seen an ad) over time.
Please note that 606 ads (out of 34283 total ads) did not have potential reach data. Almost all of those ads are from before April 2020.
