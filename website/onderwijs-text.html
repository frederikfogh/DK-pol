For each demographic, four graphs are shown that compare:

  • Occurrences: The amount of ads that were about "onderwijs".
  • Impressions: How many times ads about "onderwijs" were shown.
  • Potential reach: The maximum amount of people that ads about "onderwijs" could have been shown to.
  • Spending: The amount of money that parties spend on ads.
